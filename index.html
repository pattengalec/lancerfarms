About the Farm

Our Story

Lancer Farms & Gardens

Lancer Farms & Gardens is the working farm of California Baptist University in Riverside, CA. Students, faculty, and community volunteers cultivate specialty crops, practice sustainable agriculture, and explore the intersection of faith and stewardship of the land.

Our Mission

To educate and equip the next generation of growers, to care for creation faithfully, and to feed our community with intention and love.
